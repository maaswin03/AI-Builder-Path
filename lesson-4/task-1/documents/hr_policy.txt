Presidio HR Policy

Working Hours:
Employees are expected to work from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM.

Leave Policy:
Employees are entitled to 12 casual leaves and 12 sick leaves annually.

Work From Home:
Employees may work remotely up to 2 days per week with manager approval.

Medical Insurance:
Health insurance is provided for all full-time employees and their dependents.

Code of Conduct:
Employees must follow company ethics, maintain confidentiality, and comply with all security policies.

AI Data Handling:
Confidential company data must never be uploaded to public AI tools.
Only approved enterprise AI platforms may be used for processing internal documents.

Performance Reviews:
Performance reviews are conducted twice a year.
