# Prompt Analysis

## Original Prompt

"You are a helpful assistant. Answer the user's question about their billing issue."

## Limitations

* The assistant's role is not clearly defined.
* No instructions on tone or professionalism.
* No billing policies or constraints are provided.
* Does not specify how to handle refunds, late fees, or incorrect charges.
* Responses may be generic and inconsistent.
* No guidance on requesting missing information before answering.
