IT Support Policy

VPN Access:
Employees should install Cisco AnyConnect VPN from the company portal.

Approved Software:
Only approved software may be installed.
Software requests must be submitted through the IT Service Portal.

Laptop Requests:
Employees can request a new laptop through ServiceNow after manager approval.

Password Reset:
Employees should use the Self-Service Password Reset portal.

Email Setup:
Microsoft Outlook is the approved email client.

Antivirus:
All company devices must have Microsoft Defender enabled.
