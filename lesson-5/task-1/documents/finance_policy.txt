Finance Policy

Payroll:
Payroll is processed on the last working day of every month.

Expense Reimbursement:
Employees must submit receipts through the Expense Portal.

Travel Claims:
Travel claims must be submitted within 30 days of travel.

Budget Reports:
Monthly budget reports are available on the Finance Portal.

Purchase Orders:
All purchases above $1000 require manager approval.

Invoices:
Invoices are processed within five business days.
