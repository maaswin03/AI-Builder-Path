Company HR & IT Policy

Employees receive 12 casual leaves per year.

Employees can work from home up to 2 days per week with manager approval.

VPN access is provided using Cisco AnyConnect.

Laptop replacement is available after 3 years.

Reimbursement requests should be submitted through the Finance Portal within 30 days.

Payroll is processed on the last working day of every month.

For IT support, employees should create a ticket in the Service Desk Portal.
